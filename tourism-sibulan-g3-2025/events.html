Events & Festivals

Discover annual festivals, special events, and community activities.

Yagyag Festival

This is Sibulan's signature event, celebrating the abundance of marine life and the traditional practice of crab spawning in the local Sapa spring. It is characterized by vibrant street dancing competitions featuring crab-inspired costumes and choreography.

Buglasan Festival

While primarily a province-wide event held in nearby Dumaguete City, Sibulan participates in this "Festival of Festivals" by sending its Yagyag contingent. The event is a grand showcase of the diverse cultures and local festivals of all the municipalities within Negros Oriental.

Feast of San Antonio de Padua

Held annually on June 13, this religious festival honors the town's patron saint with a unique and well-attended fluvial (river) procession. Devotees decorate boats to carry images of the saint, highlighting the community's strong faith and coastal identity.
