About Sibulan

A brief history and highlights of the town, culture, landscape, and community.

Heritage & Culture

Sibulan’s cultural roots are shaped by centuries of tradition, faith, and community spirit. From local celebrations to ancestral customs, the town has maintained its identity through festivals, rituals, and stories passed down through generations.

Geography & Nature

Sibulan features a diverse landscape ranging from cool upland communities to scenic coastlines facing the Bohol Sea. The municipality is known for its clean environment, rich vegetation, freshwater sources, and breathtaking viewpoints.
