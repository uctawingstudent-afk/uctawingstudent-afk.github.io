Highland Goat Farm

A family-friendly farm set in rolling hills where visitors can meet and feed friendly goats, learn about sustainable small-scale farming, and try fresh dairy products. The farm offers guided tours, photo spots among the pastures, and hands-on activities perfect for school groups and families.
